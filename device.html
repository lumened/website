The Device

Integrating educational resources like YouTube or Khan Academy into classrooms isn’t possible in areas lacking electricity. At LumenEd, we are building technology that supports these activities anywhere in the world.

Previous efforts to introduce technology into schools have focused too much on gadgets and too little on teaching. Our goal was to break this habit, and design something completely from the ground-up for classrooms. Our result was a unique device built to display audiovisual content and to complement a video pen pal program.

Built to work anywhere

The Bright Orange Box allows teachers to teach, and students to learn, without worrying about technology, internet, electricity, or anything else. The device runs on an internal lithium ion battery. It can be charged from a wall outlet or with a portable solar kit, and doesn't require any special installation in the classroom. It works just as well in a modern city as in a rural village.

Designed for the classroom

The Box is designed with a classroom in mind. With a powerful projector and stereo speakers, you'll never have to worry about kids at the back of the classroom not being able to see or hear. It’s small, light, and durable case allows teachers to carry the device in and out of classrooms.

Easy to use

Our custom touchscreen interface is simple and intuitive, accessible to those with limited exposure to technology. The interface only includes the essentials, and does not require a keyboard or mouse.

Two-way street

We believe motivated learning is a two-way street. A camera and microphone on the back of the device are used for our video pen pal program, which establishes relationships between classrooms from around the world.

What's Next

While the Bright Orange Box is our proof-of-concept, we are developing a more scalable platform. This involves redesigning the device to be manufactured, to reduce costs and refine the device. We are also exploring additional technologies that could impact learning outcomes in classrooms. This will complete our vision of a powerful, cost-effective, portable, and power efficient device built from scratch for the classroom.
