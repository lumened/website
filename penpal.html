The Concept

Educational resources exist. So do classrooms that want to connect with their peers. But technology that can make these accessible to any teacher and any classrom doesn't.

Previous attempts to bring technology into the classroom have limited

The Orange Box is our Proof Of Concept. Handbuilt using retail parts, the device serves to show that technology can have a meaningful impact on learning.

Works Anywhere

The Orange Box runs on an internal lithium battery. It can be charged from a wall outlet or with a portable solar kit. It doesn't require any special installation. It works as well in a modern classroom as it does in a rural village.

All about Content

The Box puts its energy into the important things. The content needs to be bright and loud. It can be heard and seen clearly in the back of a large classroom.

Easy to Use

The box has to be easy to use for anyone. It needs to be accessible to those who have limited experience with technology. The interface does not require a keyboard. The touch control is simple and intuitive. Everything that is included is necessary. Everything unnecessary is left out.

Designed for the Classroom

We designed with the classroom in mind. It can placed on a chair or desk; propped on some books. Its visible on white board, wall or even a sheet. Its small and light enough to be moved in and out of classrooms and rugged enough to handle it.

Two Way Street

Motivated learning is a two way street. So our device is two way as well. There's a microphone and camera on the back. We're using it for our video penpal program. We're confident that's only the beginning.
