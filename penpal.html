The Program


We facilitate video conversations between classrooms across the globe.

In the twenty-first century, digital video conferencing provides students and teachers the opportunity to break down classroom walls. We allow students from different backgrounds to interact, so they discover similarities and learn from their differences. Whether the connection is across different cities or different continents, we give students the chance to see a world outside of their classroom.

Our video pen pal program encourages kids to promote critical thinking, alternative perspectives, personal expression, and creativity in a manner that has never been possible before. Further, it enables communities in the developing world to record and preserve their own culture, language and stories.

Establishing a Connection

First, we find two classrooms with students in the same grade. We introduce the teachers and encourage them to discuss what they would like to gain from the program. After initializing the program, each classroom makes a video introducing themselves. Students are encouraged to collaborate and direct conversations that include their entire class.

Our Role

In addition to providing the platform for video creation and playback, we are responsible for delivering videos between the two classrooms. We can do minor video editing but encourage simple dialogue that is carefully planned. We also offer subtitles between classrooms with language barriers, although we encourage exploring alternative forms of expression such as art or music.

Learning Opportunities

By facilitating video conversations between students, we open the door to several new and exciting opportunities for creative expression and active learning. For example, collaborating with classmates to create videos provides an avenue for thoughtful writing by connecting to an authentic audience. When students know that someone is going to read their letter and respond to it, it encourages them to write with a sense of purpose. This is only one of the many benefits to learning we can achieve by connecting classrooms through video.
