The Box

Our platform centers around a device that integrates a projector, PC and video camera. It serves as a multimedia device to play audiovisual content to an entire classroom.

Powered by a solar-charged battery, each device is capable of operating without electricity or internet access. Its portability, simplicity and ruggedness guarantees the device can be used by anyone, anywhere.

In the past, technology on its own has failed to solve education.

That's why we focus tirelessly on the relevance of our learning materials.


The Content

Each device comes preloaded with a library of educational videos on a range of core subjects, including english, science, and math.

The content mostly consists of short clips on specific topics and is mapped onto existing standards, like NCERT or the Common Core. This allows us to work with teachers to seamlessly integrate this content into their existing lesson plans.

The Pen Pal Program

Better learning involves taking students out of their classrooms and engaging them with the global community. Through the LumenEd Pen Pal Program, classrooms share their knowledge, stories and language with each other through weekly video exchanges.

Our hope is to not only facilitate interactivity in the classroom, but to give students the opportunity to engage, learn, and involve themselves in distant comm unties.
